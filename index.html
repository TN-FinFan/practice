Welcome to 16 Sunbeam Lake Loop!

Cell phone service is unreliable in the area (especially Verizon), so make sure you have directions downloaded

How to get here from I-24:

  • Take exit 127 for US-64/TN-50 toward Pelham/Winchester
  • Turn left onto TN-50 E/US-64 and continue for 11.1 miles
  • Turn right onto TN-108 S and continue for 13 miles
  • Turn left onto TN-399 E and continue for 9.1 miles
  • Sharp right onto Fredonia Rd and continue for 6.2 miles
  • Sharp right onto John Henry Lewis Rd and continue 0.9 miles to Sunshine Lake Estates
  • Inside Sunshine Lake Estates continue straight past first lake, staying to the right at the fork
  • After road forks, approximately 0.3 miles you will see a telephone pole with address sign on right side of road. Turn left here.
  • Follow trail approximately 0.3miles until it dead ends at the campsite
